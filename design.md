# Be real like post design

(there is still a lot of stuff to do). Here is my reasoning for why it looks the way it does and (some of) my thinking:

## Original Idea

Original idea was to `just make be real`. 

Unforch, we cant just copy paste it - it will not work and its missing the point.

our point: increase value we give to barbershops and to customers

## Making idea real

I converted it into sales machine that maxes out discovery and clears out all the doubt about style that customers see.

flow: 
`hook` (main photo) -> `fit breakdown` (check if hair thickness and if its not too much effort to keep) -> and finally `hair breakdown` (actual instructions for barber)

this post design answers all those questions:
- Where? 
- How far?
- Can I afford this haircut?
- Who did this haircut?
- Does the most recent haircut look good in this shop?
- Will this style fit my length and thickness?
- What does it take to maintain it? and How?
- What instructions do I give to barber?
- Who do I call?
- How do I get there

This design is not just "look at this hair or this one" - it gives confidence to user. (see cards after pictures)

> also, I know we discussed about adding likes - but they will likely hurt shops more, because almost no one will ever like haircut pictures as soon as they come out. So I replaced them with `save` button

### small details that make it even more cool:

notice ghost outlines of `person`, `spray bottle`, `scissors`; It makes it look much more premium with gold outline and additionally I have applied my photography knowledge to guide sight using those objects.
and ofc consistency, and its all custom made


### Finally

Well done to us coming up with this idea - it adds a lot of value to shops(customer conversions) and customers(real and live barbershop reviews)
